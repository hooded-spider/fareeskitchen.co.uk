Who We Are

Built on Ambition.
Delivered with Quality.

Faree's Kitchen Limited is a UK-registered ghost kitchen operator delivering restaurant-quality food experiences across multiple brands — all from a single professional kitchen. Incorporated as a Private Limited company (No. 16985185), we operate at the forefront of the UK's fast-growing delivery-only food sector.

Our model is simple but powerful: partner with proven, high-demand food brands, operate them to the highest standard, and deliver them to customers wherever they are through the UK's leading delivery platforms — Uber Eats and Deliveroo.

As a proud partner of Sessions — the UK's leading ghost kitchen platform — Faree's Kitchen benefits from world-class brand frameworks, supply chain support, and the operational infrastructure of 400+ delivery kitchens nationwide.

Our Model

Ghost Kitchen.
Real Results.

The ghost kitchen model eliminates the overheads of a traditional restaurant while delivering the same — often better — food quality. Faree's Kitchen runs a lean, efficient, multi-brand operation designed for the modern delivery economy.

01

Brand Partnership via Sessions UK

We operate under the Sessions platform — the UK's leading ghost kitchen brand distributor with 400+ partner kitchens. This gives us access to proven brand frameworks, trained recipes, supply chains, and marketing infrastructure from day one.

02

Multi-Brand Single Kitchen Efficiency

All four brands — Rüdi's, Wing Drop, Hawkins Diner, and Mikos Gyros — are prepared from one professional kitchen. This allows us to maximise kitchen throughput, reduce idle time, and keep operating costs low without sacrificing quality.

03

Order Management via Delivery Platforms

Orders come in directly through Uber Eats and Deliveroo — the UK's top two delivery platforms. Customers browse, order, and track from the same apps they already use. We handle everything from kitchen to door.

04

Quality Control & Brand Compliance

Each brand maintains its own recipe standards, packaging, and presentation guidelines. Sessions' operational tech platform monitors quality KPIs across every order — ensuring every delivery matches brand expectations.
